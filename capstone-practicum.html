Capstone Practicum

The Practicum is a culminating experience where the student prepares a final report describing, evaluating and reflecting on the Practicum experience and outcomes thereby demonstrating a deep understanding of the chosen topic and how it impacts the student. This Practicum report include the following sections:

  1. Learn
  2. Listen
  3. walk

Learn

This phase is designed to help me identify important issues facing me, a friend or a loved one at this time in my life! For this phase of the practicum, I studied and found quotes/statements on five (5) identified topics that are the current prophetic emphases to young adults, as well as, three (3) additional topics that are of personal interest to me.

The Lord has taught us that “whether by mine own voice or by the voice of my servants, it is the same” (D&C 1:38). Additionally, he has promised that if we “give heed unto all [the prophets] words and commandments … the gates of hell shall not prevail against you; yea, and the Lord God will disperse the powers of darkness from before you, and cause the heavens to shake for your good” (D&C 21:4-6).

What are the living prophets saying? For this part of the practicum, I collected a minimum of 10 quotes or statements, of “counsel” and “words of divine wisdom” from the living prophets for each of my 8 topics (for a minimum of 80 total quotes). I have chosen statements from the living prophets that help me better understand what the Lord and His servants teach concerning the topic, as well as possible ways I can more fully submit to God’s mind and will. To read the quotes I collected for each of the 8 topics, click on the button on each card below.
