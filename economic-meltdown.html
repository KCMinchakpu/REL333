Capstone Practicum: Listen

The following 10 quotes were gathered by me from the teaching of Prophets, Seers, and Revelators for each topic. 1- 6 are from the current living prophets and apostles—with no more than 2 quotes used from the same talk on the same topic. Quotes 7 – 10 are from other prophets, apostles or speaker in an authorized setting—BYU-Idaho or BYU-Provo Devotional; Liahona, Ensign or New Era Magazine; etc.—from this dispensation! Church leader quotes only!. Each quote include a FULL reference of speaker, talk title, and date given. The References are on a line by themselves. And each has its own reference.

Economic meltdown, Wars and Natural disasters

Quote #1:"The Church is well known for being among the first responders when tragedy strikes."

President Russell M. Nelson, "The Second Great Commandment", October 2019

Quote #2:"In these times of worldwide turmoil, more and more persons of faith are turning to the Lord for blessings of comfort and healing."

Elder Dallin H. Oaks, "Healing the Sick", April 2010

Quote #3:"When the storms in life come, you can be steady because you are standing on the rock of your faith in Jesus Christ."

President Henry B. Eyring, "Steady in the Storms" April 2022

Quote #4:"Economic deprivation is a curse that keeps on cursing, year after year and generation after generation. It damages bodies, maims spirits, harms families, and destroys dreams. If we could do more to alleviate poverty, as Jesus repeatedly commands us to do, maybe some of the less fortunate in the world could hum a few notes of 'There Is Sunshine in My Soul Today,' perhaps for the first time in their lives."

Elder Jeffrey R. Holland, "Songs Sung and Unsung", April 2017

Quote #5:"We live, brothers and sisters, in the days preceding the Lord’s Second Coming, a time long anticipated by believers through the ages. We live in days of wars and rumors of wars, days of natural disasters, days when the world is pulled by confusion and commotion. We rejoice in these days and pray that we will be able to courageously face our struggles and uncertainties."

Elder Neil L. Andersen, "Thy Kingdom Come", April 2015

Quote #6:"We know from the scriptures that some trials are for our good and are suited for our own personal development."

Elder Quentin L. Cook, "“Hope Ya Know, We Had a Hard Time” October 2008

Quote #7:"We recognize that we are living in a time of turmoil, disaster, and war. We and many others feel strongly the great need for a 'defense, and for a refuge from the storm...Our safety lies in the virtue of our lives. Our strength lies in our righteousness'".

Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf, "Christlike Attributes—the Wind beneath Our Wings", October 2005

Quote #8:"Members of the Church are feeling the economic pinch of higher taxes and inflation coupled with conditions of continuing recession.... We encourage you to be more self-reliant...The Lord wants us to be independent and self-reliant because these will be days of tribulation. He has warned and forewarned us of the eventuality."

President Ezra Taft Benson, "Prepare for the Days of Tribulation", October 1980

Quote #9:"... I’d like us to turn our thoughts and our attitudes away from the troubles around us and to focus instead on our blessings as members of the Church."

President Thomas S. Monson, "Be of Good Cheer" April 2009

Quote #10:"The lasting effects of economic challenges are often determined by our attitude toward life."

Elder James E. Faust, "The Blessings We Receive As We Meet the Challenges of Economic Stress" October 1982
