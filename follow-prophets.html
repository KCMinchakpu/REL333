Capstone Practicum: Listen

The following 10 quotes were gathered by me from the teaching of Prophets, Seers, and Revelators for each topic. 1- 6 are from the current living prophets and apostles—with no more than 2 quotes used from the same talk on the same topic. Quotes 7 – 10 are from other prophets, apostles or speaker in an authorized setting—BYU-Idaho or BYU-Provo Devotional; Liahona, Ensign or New Era Magazine; etc.—from this dispensation! Church leader quotes only!. Each quote include a FULL reference of speaker, talk title, and date given. The References are on a line by themselves. And each has its own reference.

Let God Prevail & Follow His Prophet

Quote #1: "Regardless of where you live or what your circumstances are, the Lord Jesus Christ is your Savior, and God’s prophet Joseph Smith is your prophet."

President Russell M. Nelson “Let God Prevail” October 2020

Quote #2: "We have been blessed abundantly in this general conference to learn about eternal principles from the Lord’s authorized servants. Now, our individual responsibility is to govern ourselves according to the truths of which they have testified."

Elder David A. Bednar, “The Principles of My Gospel”, April 2021

Quote #3: “A prophet does not stand between you and the Savior. Rather, he stands beside you and points the way to the Savior.”

Elder Neil L. Andersen, “The Prophet of God” April 2018

Quote #4: "Throughout history, a loving Father in Heaven and His Son, Jesus Christ, who is the head of the Church, have blessed us with prophets who counsel and warn about future dangers"

Elder Quentin L. Cook, "Give Heed unto the Prophets’ Words", April 2008

Quote #5: "Our Father is willing to guide each of us along His covenant path with steps designed to our individual need and tailored to His plan for our ultimate happiness with Him."

Elder D. Todd Christofferson, "Our Relationship with God", April 2022

Quote #6: "At the heart of God’s plan for your happiness is your power to choose. Of course, your Heavenly Father wants you to choose eternal joy with Him, and He will help you to achieve it, but He would never force it upon you."

Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf, "Jesus Christ Is the Strength of Youth", October 2022

Quote #7: "President Russell M. Nelson taught us the importance of putting Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ first in our lives. President Nelson taught us that one meaning of the word Israel is 'let God prevail.'”

President M. Russell Ballard, “Lovest Thou Me More Than These?”, October 2021

Quote #8: “Believe in the Lord your God, so shall ye be established; believe his prophets, so shall ye prosper.”

President Gordon B. Hinckley, “Believe His Prophets”, April 1992

Quote #9: "Great temporal and spiritual strength flows from following those who have the keys of the kingdom of God in our time. Personal strength and power result from obedience to eternal principles taught by the living legates of the Lord."

Elder James E. Faust, "The Keys That Never Rust", October 2022

Quote #10: "The Saints today need instruction from God through his prophets just as did the members of the ancient church."

Elder Mark E. Petersen, "Follow the Prophets", October 1981 General Conference
