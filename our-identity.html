Capstone Practicum: Listen

The following 10 quotes were gathered by me from the teaching of Prophets, Seers, and Revelators for each topic. 1- 6 are from the current living prophets and apostles—with no more than 2 quotes used from the same talk on the same topic. Quotes 7 – 10 are from other prophets, apostles or speaker in an authorized setting—BYU-Idaho or BYU-Provo Devotional; Liahona, Ensign or New Era Magazine; etc.—from this dispensation! Church leader quotes only!. Each quote include a FULL reference of speaker, talk title, and date given. The References are on a line by themselves. And each has its own reference.

Our Divine Identity

Quote #1: “Each of us has a divine potential because each is a child of God. Each is equal in His eyes. The implications of this truth are profound.”

President Russell M. Nelson “Let God Prevail” October 2020

Quote #2: “The gospel teaches us that we are the spirit children of heavenly parents. Before our mortal birth we had “a pre-existent, spiritual personality, as the sons and daughters of the Eternal Father.”

Elder Dallin H. Oaks “The Great Plan of Happiness” October 1993

Quote #3: “Brothers and sisters, I humbly testify that as we attend the temple, we can be reminded of the eternal nature of our spirits, our relationship with the Father and His divine Son, and our ultimate desire to return to our heavenly home.”

President Henry B. Eyring “All Will Be Well Because of Temple Covenants” April 2024

Quote #4: “Heavenly Father’s great plan of happiness includes the doctrine, the ordinances, the covenants, and the exceeding great and precious promises whereby we can become partakers of the divine nature”

Elder David A. Bednar, “Exceeding Great and Precious Promises.” October 2017

Quote #5: “I invite you to center your life on Jesus Christ and remember the foundational truths in the Young Women theme”

Elder Dale G. Renlund. “Your Divine Nature and Eternal Destiny” April 2022

Quote #6: “The Lord’s hand is guiding you by divine design. He is in the small details of your life as well as the major milestones.”

Elder Ronald A. Rasband “Divine Design” October 2017

Quote #7: “One title that defines all of us in the most fundamental way is son [and daughter] of Heavenly Father. No matter what else we are or do in life, we must never forget that we are God’s literal spirit children.”

President Dieter F. Uchtdorf “Four Titles” April 2013

Quote #8: “As sons and daughters of the covenant, we can help to eliminate this kind of behavior by looking at the apparent differences that exist between us with the Savior’s eyes and based on what we have in common—our divine identity and kinship.”

Elder Ulisses Soares “Brothers and Sisters in Christ” October 2023

Quote #9: “Knowing that we are loved by our Heavenly Father will help us know who we are and know that we belong to His great eternal family… Because we are the spirit children of God, everyone has a divine origin, nature, and potential. Each of us ‘is a beloved spirit son or daughter of heavenly parents.’ This is our identity! This is who we really are!”

President M. Russell Ballard “Hope in Christ” April 2021

Quote #10: “You are a child of God. He is the Father of your spirit. Spiritually you are of noble birth, the offspring of the King of Heaven. Fix that truth in your mind and hold to it.”

Elder Boyd K. Packer “To Young Women and Men” April 1989
