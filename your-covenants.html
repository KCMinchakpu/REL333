Capstone Practicum: Listen

The following 10 quotes were gathered by me from the teaching of Prophets, Seers, and Revelators for each topic. 1- 6 are from the current living prophets and apostles—with no more than 2 quotes used from the same talk on the same topic. Quotes 7 – 10 are from other prophets, apostles or speaker in an authorized setting—BYU-Idaho or BYU-Provo Devotional; Liahona, Ensign or New Era Magazine; etc.—from this dispensation! Church leader quotes only!. Each quote include a FULL reference of speaker, talk title, and date given. The References are on a line by themselves. And each has its own reference.

The Power of Jesus Christ and Your Covenants

Quote #1: “Faith in Jesus Christ propels us to do things we otherwise would not do. Faith that motivates us to action gives us more access to His power."

President Russell M. Nelson “Let God Prevail” October 2020

Quote #2: "Because of His Atonement, the Savior has the power to succor—to help—every mortal pain and affliction."

Elder Dallin H. Oaks “The Great Plan of Happiness” October 1993

Quote #3: "Go forward in faith in keeping your covenants with God and so claim the promise He has made to you with an oath."

President Henry B. Eyring “All Will Be Well Because of Temple Covenants” April 2024

Quote #4: "In times of distress, let your covenants be paramount and let your obedience be exact."

Elder D. Todd Christofferson "The Power of Covenants" April 2019

Quote #5: "As you walk the covenant path, from baptism to the temple and throughout life, I promise you power to go against the natural worldly flow."

Elder Dale G. Renlund. “Your Divine Nature and Eternal Destiny” April 2022

Quote #6: "I invite you to consider the promises and covenants you make with the Lord, and with others, with great integrity, knowing that your word is your bond."

Elder Ronald A. Rasband “Divine Design” October 2017

Quote #7: "Hope is a gift of the Spirit. It is a hope that through the Atonement of Jesus Christ and the power of His Resurrection, we shall be raised unto life eternal and this because of our faith in the Savior."

President Dieter F. Uchtdorf “Four Titles” April 2013

Quote #8: "When we enter the Lord’s house, we embark on a sacred journey of learning to become higher and holier disciples of Christ."

Elder Ulisses Soares “Brothers and Sisters in Christ” October 2023

Quote #9: "You must be honest with yourself and remain true to the covenants you have made with God. Do not fall into the trap of thinking you can sin a little and it will not matter."

President M. Russell Ballard “Hope in Christ” April 2021

Quote #10: "I have since reflected much on the power of Christ to heal and bless. It was He who said, 'I am come that they might have life, and that they might have it more abundantly.' In a world of sickness and sorrow, of tension and jealousy and greed, there must be much of healing if there is to be life abundant."

President Gordon B. Hinckley "The Healing Power of Christ" October 1988
