Capstone Practicum: Listen

The following 10 quotes were gathered by me from the teaching of Prophets, Seers, and Revelators for each topic. 1- 6 are from the current living prophets and apostles—with no more than 2 quotes used from the same talk on the same topic. Quotes 7 – 10 are from other prophets, apostles or speaker in an authorized setting—BYU-Idaho or BYU-Provo Devotional; Liahona, Ensign or New Era Magazine; etc.—from this dispensation! Church leader quotes only!. Each quote include a FULL reference of speaker, talk title, and date given. The References are on a line by themselves. And each has its own reference.

Take Charge of Your Testimony

Quote #1:“Seeking to answer these questions will require effort—much effort. I plead with you to take charge of your testimony. Work for it. Own it. Care for it. Nurture it so that it will grow. Feed it truth. Don’t pollute it with the false philosophies of unbelieving men and women and then wonder why your testimony is waning.”

President Russell M. Nelson, “Choices for Eternity”, May 2022

Quote #2: “First, we can teach some sacred truth. Then we can testify that we know what we have taught is true. And then we must act so that those who hear our testimony see that our actions conform with what we said was true. The Holy Ghost will then confirm to them the truth of what we said and that we knew it to be true."

Elder Henry B. Eyring, "A Legacy of Testimony", April 1996

Quote #3: "...we can be blessed by the Holy Ghost to receive an individual and spiritual stillness of the soul that enables us to know and remember that God is our Heavenly Father, we are His children, Jesus Christ is our Savior, and we can be blessed to do and overcome hard things."

Elder David A. Bednar, “Be Still, and Know That I Am God”, April 2024

Quote #4: "Your testimony is a most precious possession, often associated with deep spiritual feelings. These feelings are usually communicated quietly and described as a ‘still small voice.’ It is your belief or knowledge of truth given as a spiritual witness through the influence of the Holy Ghost. Acquiring this witness will change what you say and how you act."

Elder Gary E. Stevenson, "Nourishing and Bearing Your Testimony", October 2022

Quote #5: "By identifying our ancestors and performing for them the saving ordinances they could not themselves perform, we are testifying of the infinite reach of the Atonement of Jesus Christ."

Elder D. Todd Christofferson, "The Redemption of the Dead and the Testimony of Jesus", October 2000

Quote #6: "...individual religious observance is more likely to develop when your family religious observance makes it clear to your children that all other life goals, such as occupation and education, are secondary to having a testimony of the Savior and living righteously.”

Elder Quentin L. Cook, “A Willing Heart and Mind”, April 2008

Quote #7: "I now invite you to take your know-how, coupled with your time-honored testimonies, and go on a mission."

Elder Ronald A. Rasband, "How Great Will Be Your Joy", October 2023

Quote #8: “You can share your testimony in many ways—by the words you speak, by the example you set, by the manner in which you live your life."

President Thomas S. Monson, "Be Thou an Example", April 2005

Quote #9: "If you have the Holy Spirit resting upon you, and you speak by that Spirit the words of the Lord as contained in the holy scriptures and as outlined by his living prophets, God will ratify your message in the hearts of those who are hearing you."

President James E. Faust, "What I Want My Son to Know before He Leaves on His Mission" April 1996

Quote #10:“Having a testimony alone is not enough. As our conversion to Jesus Christ grows, we naturally want to testify of Him-His goodness, love, and kindness."

President M. Russell Ballard, "Remember What Matters Most", April 2023
